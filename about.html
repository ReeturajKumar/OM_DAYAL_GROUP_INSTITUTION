    ACHEIVEMENTS

    As a NAAC accredited institution, the college offers engineering and architecture courses affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), formerly known as WBUT. The Engineering and Architecture courses are approved by AICTE and COA respectively to ensure that students are able to build a strong knowledge and application foundation with the guidance of experienced faculty and a robust learning environment. OmDayal Group of Institutions, one of the best engineering and architecture college in Kolkata, continuously upgrades their teaching and learning practices to help students develop top-notch analytical, designing, communication, and problem-solving skills, as new age technology professionals.
